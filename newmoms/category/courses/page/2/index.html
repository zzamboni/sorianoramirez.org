Baby swimming class

Tuesday, September 26th, 2006

Babys im Wasser, Postfach 184 6318 Walchwil

Advantages:

This course gives the baby the opportunity to experience another environment as the one she/he is used to. It also helps the parents or mothers to overcome their fear of their baby being in water. Which opens a lot of possibilities for fun things to do with your baby.

Disadvantages:

The instructor does not seem very enthusiastic most of the time. The course could be even more interesting and enjoyable for the babies if the atmosphere were more engaging.
